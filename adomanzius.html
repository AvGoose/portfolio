ADOMANZIUS

ANIMATION IN 3D



These animation loops were made using the open-source software Blender 3D. Check out my Instagram for recent updates here. Adomanzius is a side-project I like to do for fun while learning 3D-modeling, animation, and video editing. These loops are made specifically for Instagram, with the intent of them being perfect loops in the app. They're also great for backgrounds such as Spotify Canvas.
